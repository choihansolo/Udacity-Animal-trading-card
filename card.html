Sea otter

90% of all sea otters live on the coast of Alaska and they eat 25-38% of their body weight in food everyday.

  • Scientific Name: Enhydra lutris
  • Average weight/length: 49-99lb / 3'11''-4'11''
  • Average Lifespan: 10-15 yrs for males, 15-20 yrs for females
  • Diet: Marine benthic invertebrates

Sea otters spend much of their lives in the water and can dive up to 330 feet when foraging for food. They sometimes rest in coastal kelp forests, often draping the kelp over their bodies to keep from drifting away.
